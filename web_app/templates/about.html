About The Application

How To Use:

Users may input a ticker symbol for any publicly traded stock.

About:

After a ticker has been selected, the system will then analyze the 100 most recent news headlines for that company and generate a median investor sentiment score for the stock for each given day.

Scoring:

Stocks are given a daily median score ranging from -1 to 1.

-1 = Negative Sentiment: Do not invest

0 = Neutral Sentiment: Further sources should be considered before investing

1 = Positive Sentiment: Proceed with investment
