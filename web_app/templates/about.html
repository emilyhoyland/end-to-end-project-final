About The Application

A user inputs a ticker symbol for a publicly traded stock. The system will then analyze the median investor sentiment score based on the most recent 100 news headlines.

This range of median scores is from -1 to 1.

-1= negative sentiment, do not investment

0 = nuetral sentiment

1 = positive sentiment, proceed with investment
